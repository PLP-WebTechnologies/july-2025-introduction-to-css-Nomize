Whispers of Dawn

The sun spills gold upon the waking earth,
Birds trace songs in the soft-pink air,
And every leaf turns to greet the light,
Whispering secrets only the morning knows.

In Your Orbit

You are the moon in my midnight sky,
Pulling tides in the quiet parts of me,
I circle you endlessly, caught between
The gravity of love and the light of longing.

The Ocean’s Reply

I asked the sea if it missed the shore,
It answered in waves, folding over my feet,
Telling me love always returns
In ways the heart will understand.
